Classic Mac and Cheese

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb macaroni pasta
  • 4 cups water (for boiling pasta)
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 3 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Breadcrumbs (optional, for topping)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a large pot, bring water to a boil. Add macaroni and cook until al dente, about 8 minutes; drain.
  3. In a separate saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Stir in flour to create a roux, cook for about a minute.
  4. Slowly pour in milk, whisking constantly to prevent lumps. Continue cooking until sauce thickens, about 5 minutes.
  5. Add salt, pepper, and paprika. Stir in 2 1/2 cups of cheddar cheese and all the mozzarella until smooth.
  6. Mix in the drained macaroni and stir until fully coated with the cheese sauce.
  7. Transfer the macaroni and cheese sauce to a baking dish. Sprinkle the top with the remaining cheddar cheese and breadcrumbs if desired.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is bubbly and topping is browned, about 25 minutes.
